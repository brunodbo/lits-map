Line In The Sand is a collaborative multimedia project which aims to convey the nuanced opinions and personal stories of individuals living along the route of the proposed Northern Gateway pipeline. Begun in the summer of 2012, we have travelled from Bruderheim, Alberta, to Kitimat, British Columbia, collecting testimonies from countless Canadians whose land, livelihoods, and cultures could be profoundly impacted by the approval and construction of the Northern Gateway.
